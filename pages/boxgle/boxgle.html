I wouldn't say that I was an IPad kid. After all, generation alpha was way after I was born. However, I will say that people blame generation alpha for being IPad kids way too much. When you have a cool tablet in front of you and you're a kid, you are going to want to play with it. You're definitely going to download some type of game at some point of time and get hooked on it. The game I played had you move around as a smaller square while being chased after by larger red squares, which would always move in a certain pattern, making the game a little bit skill-based. After many years, I have completely forgotten what the original name of the game was. I only remember having a lot of fun playing it with my friends in school. Thus, as a bit of a tribute (because the game could have very well been removed from the IOS app store along time ago) and practice with programming, I made Boxgle.
