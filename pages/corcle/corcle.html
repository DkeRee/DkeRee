Keep in mind that there IS NO DELTATIME for this game, meaning that it will run at different speeds proportional to your frames per second or the refresh rate of your monitor!

PLAY

There are only SIX levels in this game, but the last three are excruciatingly difficult. Just like Wanshot, you will have infinite lives, but in true fashion of a rage game, I have put a death counter on the top of your screen. The idea to get through each level is very simple. You can touch the white blocks. You cannot touch the red blocks. The orange pads launch you, and the blue ones teleport you. Though, do watch out, there is a level in the game that tricks you.
